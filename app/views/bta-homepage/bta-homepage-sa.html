Self-Employment Income Support Scheme (SEISS)

You can now claim a grant from the Self-Employment Income Support Scheme (SEISS) (opens in a new window or tab).

You need to be an eligible self-employed individual or a member of a partnership that has lost income because of coronavirus.

Delay July's Self Assessment payment

Your second Self Assessment payment on account for the tax year 2019 to 2020 is due by 31 July 2020. If you cannot pay because of coronavirus you can delay (defer) your Self Assessment payment until 31 January 2021 (opens in new tab). You do not need to apply or tell HMRC: it will automatically count as a deferral if you do not pay. You’ll need to cancel your Direct Debit if you set one up for July.

If you do not pay it all by 31 January 2021, you may get a penalty and interest charges on any unpaid amounts.
