What assistants can do

As an assistant, you can:

  • complete and submit tax returns
  • make payments
  • view balance and payment history
  • access messages

You cannot:

  • add or remove taxes, duties or schemes
  • manage who can access this account
  • update account details and contact information

If you need to do any of these things, the person who set up your account can change your role to administrator.
