Coronavirus (COVID-19) VAT deferral

The Chancellor has announced that the Government is deferring tax payments, through the VAT system, to help businesses with their cash flows during this extraordinary time of uncertainty.

This means that any VAT payments due between March 20 and 30 June inclusive can be deferred if you need to retain the money to support your business cash flow.

During this time, no businesses will have to make a payment for VAT to HMRC if they cannot afford to.

You do not need to tell HMRC that you are not going to make a payment.

As you normally pay by direct debit you need to cancel your direct debit with your bank.

Please do so in sufficient time so that HMRC do not attempt to automatically collect on receipt of your VAT return.

You must continue to make VAT declarations to the usual timescales.
