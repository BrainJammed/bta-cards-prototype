Delay July's Self Assessment payment

Your second Self Assessment payment on account for the tax year April 6 2019 to April 5 2020 is due by 31 July 2020. If you cannot pay because of coronavirus you can delay (defer) your Self Assessment payment until 31 January 2021 (opens in new tab).
