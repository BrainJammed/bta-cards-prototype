You need to create a goods movement reference each time you make a crossing in either direction between the EU and Great Britain or between Great Britain and Northern Ireland.

You can create a draft goods movement reference and return to complete it within 28 days. If you do not make any updates within 28 days, your goods movement reference will expire and you cannot use it.
