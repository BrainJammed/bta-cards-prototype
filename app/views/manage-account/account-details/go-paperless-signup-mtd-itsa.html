Get tax letters online

What you get

  • Self Assessment or Making Tax Digital for Income Tax legal notices, penalty notices and tax letters sent to your business tax account
  • P2 notices of coding sent to your personal tax account

When you get Self Assessment or Making Tax Digital for Income Tax notices or letters from HMRC, you must read them carefully and act on them. You may get a penalty if you do not.

We will email you when a new notice or letter has been sent to your personal or business tax account.

What you do not get

  • All your tax letters online. You will continue to get other tax letters from HMRC by post
