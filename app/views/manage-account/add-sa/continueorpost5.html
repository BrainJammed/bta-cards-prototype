We need to ask some questions to prove your identity

Details we ask for
  • UK passport
  • payslips or P60
  • Self Assessment
  • tax credits and Voice ID

You may also be able to answer questions about your credit record.

If you cannot answer these questions, we will not be able to prove your identity online. You will need to request a secure pin to be sent to you by post.

We ask these questions to protect you against malicious or fraudulent attempts to access the service.
