Check if you can register for VAT online

You cannot register online if you:

  • need to apply for a ‘registration exception’
  • are joining the Agricultural Flat Rate Scheme
  • are registering the divisions or business units of a body corporate under separate VAT numbers
  • have an EU business ‘distance selling’ to the UK
  • have imported (‘acquired’) goods worth more than £85,000 from another EU company
  • are disposing of assets on which 8th or 13th Directive refunds have been claimed
