How to pay your Corporation Tax

Deadlines

The deadline for your payment will depend on your taxable profits.

If your taxable profits are up to £1.5 million

You must pay your Corporation Tax 9 months and 1 day after the end of your accounting period. Your accounting period is usually your financial year, but you may have 2 accounting periods in the year you set up your company (opens in new tab).

If your taxable profits are more than £1.5 million

You must pay your Corporation Tax in instalments (opens in new tab).

Make sure you pay HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) by the deadline. They may charge you interest (opens in new tab) if you do not pay on time. They will pay you interest (opens in new tab) if you pay your tax early.

Ways to pay

You can no longer pay at the Post Office. You cannot pay Corporation Tax by post.

The time you need to allow depends on how you pay.

If you are paying your bill the same or the next day you can use:

  • online or telephone banking (opens in new tab) (Faster Payments)
