Changing your phone number and other sign in details

You can view or change your phone number, name, password and email address by managing your Government Gateway details.

You can use your phone number or an authenticator app to get access codes for your business tax account.

We recommend that you set up at least 1 backup device for your access codes.

You can view or change your security preferences in your Government Gateway details.

Changes take up to 2 days to update.

Changing your business details

You can view or change:

  • your phone number, company name, address and similar details in your business tax account details

  • your contact preferences between paper letters and online emails

You must change your details for each tax separately. These details do not automatically match your Government Gateway details.

Changes take up to 2 days to update.

Changing team member details as an administrator

As an administrator on a business tax account you can manage Government Gateway details for a standard user.

You can:

  • reset their password
  • remove their security preferences
  • resend their Government Gateway user ID
  • delete their Government Gateway user ID

If an administrator removes a standard user’s security preferences, the standard user must add a new phone number or authenticator app to get access codes. If they do not, they will not be able to sign in again.

To change or remove an administrator’s phone number or authenticator app, you must call HMRC.
