Getting evidence of your income - SA302

If you complete your return online you can:

  • get evidence of your earnings (SA302) for the last 4 years that you filed online
  • get a tax year overview for any year
  • print your calculation and tax year overview

You might be asked for these documents as evidence of your income, for example if you are applying for a mortgage and you are self-employed.

Check that your mortgage provider accepts documents you have printed yourself.

You cannot print your documents until up to 72 hours after you have sent your tax return.
