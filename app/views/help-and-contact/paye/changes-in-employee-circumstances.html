Changes in employee circumstances

Get maternity or other parental leave pay in advance

You must allow your employees to take paid paternal leave if they have children or adopt.

You can reclaim the payments from HMRC after you've made them.

You can ask HMRC to refund you before you make the payments.

Sending employees to work abroad

Complete form CA3821 the first time you send employees.

And complete form CA3822 for each individual employee you send.

Get HMRC to pay for an employee tax refund

If an employee has paid too much PAYE you may need to refund them.

Take the refund out of your next payment to HMRC.

Or ask HMRC to reimburse you if the refund is bigger than your next payment.
