View, check or correct your submissions

When you can view your submissions

Submissions show up in your balance at different times depending on when you submit them.

If you make a Full Payment Submission (FPS) on time, your balance will be updated by the 12th of the next month.

If you make a FPS late, see the GOV.UK guide on payroll (opens in new tab) .

If we receive an Employer Payment Summary (EPS) between the 20th and the 5th of the month, your balance will be updated by the 12th of the next month.

If we receive an EPS between the 6th and the 19th of the month, your balance will be updated within 1 day.

Your payments to HMRC

Payments you made to HMRC can take up to 6 working days to appear in your balance.

Payroll software confirmations

You can also check or view submissions in HMRC's Basic PAYE Tools (BPT) software.

To check if HMRC received a submission, select 'View successful submissions' from your employer homepage.

To view a specific submission, follow these instructions in the Basic PAYE tools user guide (opens in new tab), but select 'View' instead of 'Change'.

Contact your software provider if you do not use BPT.

Correct a PAYE submission

You must use your payroll software to correct Full Payment Submissions (FPSs) or Employer Payment Summaries (EPSs). HMRC staff cannot make changes for you.

Submissions for the current tax year

You can make changes to submissions for the current tax year until 19 April.

Follow the Basic PAYE tools user guide (opens in new tab) if you use HMRC's Basic PAYE Tools software (BPT).

Contact your software provider if you do not use BPT.

Submissions from an earlier tax year

If you spot an error in a submission after the tax year has ended, you need to submit an 'Earlier Year Update' if it is after 19 April.

You can submit an Earlier Year Update using Basic PAYE Tools (opens in new tab) if you use BPT to manage your payroll.

If you use commercial software to manage your payroll, check with your software provider whether it can process Earlier Year Updates.

If your software doesn't offer Earlier Year Updates, you will need to install BPT to submit an Earlier Year Update (opens in new tab).
