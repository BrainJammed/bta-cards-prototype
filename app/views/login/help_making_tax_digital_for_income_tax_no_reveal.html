Help with Making Tax Digital for Income Tax

Making Tax Digital For Income Tax

Making Tax Digital for Income Tax is a new system HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC) uses to collect Income Tax.

Income Tax is usually deducted automatically from wages, pensions and savings.

However, people and businesses with other income must report Income Tax by submitting quarterly updates and an annual final declaration

Making Tax Digital for Income Tax is used by:

  • the self-employed
  • sole traders
  • individuals such as company directors
  • partners in partnerships
  • trustees
  • other similar people

Self Assessment and Making Tax Digital for Income Tax

Self Assessment is the system HMRC currently uses to collect Income Tax.

After you sign up for Making Tax Digital for Income Tax, you must still submit a Self Assessment tax return for the tax year before the current one.

You must then send quarterly updates (opens in new tab) and a final declaration for Making Tax Digital for Income Tax (opens in new tab) for the current tax year.

This replaces your annual Self Assessment tax return.

You do not need to pay Income Tax quarterly. Your Income Tax is due every 31 January after the tax year ends. This is the same as Self Assessment.

Example: Changing from Self Assessment to Making Tax Digital for Income Tax

Jay Smith earns money from self-employment and reports the earnings to HMRC using Self Assessment.

In September 2021, Jay Smith decides to stop Self Assessment and sign up for Making Tax Digital for Income Tax.

By 31 January 2022, Jay must:

  • send HMRC a Self Assessment tax return for tax year 2020 to 2021
  • pay HMRC the Income Tax due for tax year 2020 to 2021

Jay must also start sending Making Tax Digital for Income Tax quarterly updates from September 2022. These updates are for the tax year 2021 to 2022.

By 31 January 2023, Jay must also:

  • send HMRC a Making Tax Digital for Income Tax final declaration for tax year 2021 to 2022
  • pay HMRC the Income Tax due for tax year 2021 to 2022

Signing up for Making Tax Digital for Income Tax

After you sign up for Making Tax Digital for Income Tax (opens in new tab) you can add it to your business tax account.

This makes it easy for you to manage Making Tax Digital for Income Tax online with your other business taxes.

You may need to have items that prove your identity to add Making Tax Digital for Income Tax to your account.

Getting Making Tax Digital for Income Tax software

You must use software to send your Making Tax Digital for Income Tax quarterly updates and final declaration.
